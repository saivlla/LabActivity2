Call of Duty : Mobile

As a first-person shooter, Call of Duty places the player in control of an infantry soldier who makes use of various authentic World War II firearms in combat.

5 reasons why this game is my favorite:

  • Its the only mobile game I play.
  • You can join and play the game with your friends.
  • I can easily make friends within the online world.
  • Its addicting to shoot people.
  • Game settings and controls are easy to understand.
